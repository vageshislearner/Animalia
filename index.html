Frequently Asked Questions

How can I adopt a pet?

Adopting a pet is easy through our platform! Visit our adoption page to browse available animals, fill out an adoption application, and schedule a meet-and-greet with your potential new family member. Our adoption process includes:

  • Application review (1-2 business days)
  • Virtual or in-person meeting with the pet
  • Home check (virtual or in-person)
  • Adoption fee payment and paperwork

How do I become a volunteer?

We'd love to have you join our volunteer team! Here's how to get started:

  1. Complete our online volunteer application
  2. Attend a virtual orientation session
  3. Choose your preferred volunteer activities (fostering, events, transports, etc.)
  4. Complete any required training for your role

Most volunteers commit 5-10 hours per month. No experience is necessary - just a love for animals!

Was this helpful?
Can I foster an animal before adopting?

Absolutely! Our foster-to-adopt program is perfect for people who want to:

  • See if a pet is the right fit for their home
  • Help an animal recover from medical procedures
  • Provide temporary care while the pet waits for adoption

Many of our foster families end up adopting their foster pets (we call this "foster fails" in the best way!). All supplies and medical care are provided during the foster period.

Was this helpful?
What should I do if I find a stray animal?

If you encounter a stray animal, please follow these steps:

  1. Assess the situation for immediate danger
  2. If safe, check for identification tags
  3. Contact local animal control or our rescue hotline
  4. If you can safely transport the animal, take them to a vet to check for a microchip
  5. Consider temporary fostering if no owner is found

Never put yourself in danger when trying to help an animal. Our team can assist with proper capture and transport if needed.
