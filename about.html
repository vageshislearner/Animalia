Our Compassionate Mission

Dedicated to creating a world where every animal receives the love and care they deserve

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Animalia Rescue began as a small group of animal lovers determined to make a difference. What started as a grassroots effort has grown into a comprehensive animal welfare organization serving our entire region.

Our journey has been marked by countless rescues, heartwarming adoptions, and an ever-growing community of supporters who share our vision for a more compassionate world.

Our Facility

In 2018, we opened our state-of-the-art animal care facility, designed specifically to provide a comfortable, stress-free environment for animals in transition.

The facility includes medical suites, rehabilitation areas, and spacious living quarters that help animals recover and prepare for their forever homes.

Our Core Principles

Mission

To rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals in need while promoting compassion through community education.

Vision

A world where every animal is valued and protected, and no pet suffers from neglect or abandonment.

Values

Compassion, Integrity, Innovation, and Community guide every decision we make.
